Now that you're signed in, here are some fun things you can do:

👍 Thumb-up songs you like

You can 👍 songs you like (and 👎 those you don't!) Chavah learns what you like and plays more of the good stuff. Thumbing-up a song increases its ranking, and songs with higher ranking are played more often for everyone. Read more...

🎶 Do a song request

Got a song you want to hear? 🎵 Request a song and it'll play for you and everyone listening. It'll play instantly for you, and everyone else when their current song finishes.

To request a song, click the button (it's next to the play/pause button), then type the name of a song, artist, or album. Read more...

💯 Share your favorite songs on Facebook

Found great song that you want your friends to hear? 😍 Share the song to Facebook, Twitter, WhatsApp, and more. You can also share the song in a text message from your phone. And if you're a blogger or run your own website, you can also embed a song right into your webpage or blog post. Spread Messiah's music around! 🙌 Read more...
